About The Brand

A Legacy Written in Stone and Story

In 1989, visionary hotelier Rajan Krishnamurthy set out to create something India had never quite seen before — a collection of luxury properties that felt less like hotels and more like homes you happened to love deeply. Beginning with a single heritage bungalow in Coorg, Veranda Hotels & Resorts has grown into a curated family of twelve properties spanning pristine coastlines, misty mountain ridges, and ancient heritage cities.

Every Veranda property is designed with a philosophy of low-rise, low-footprint architecture — buildings that belong to their landscapes rather than impose upon them. Biodiverse gardens, local artisan craftsmanship, and a fierce commitment to regional cuisine define the experience. Guests don't check into a Veranda property; they arrive home to a place they've never been before.

Locations

From Sea-Level to the Stars

Pristine white beaches where the Arabian Sea laps at ancient Portuguese forts. Tea-garden valleys where morning mist rolls over emerald hillsides. Desert palaces where centuries of Rajput history echo through every carved sandstone column. Veranda's twelve properties are deliberately spread to cover India's most extraordinary natural and heritage landscapes.

Whether you seek the thundering silence of the Himalayas at Shimla, the salt-and-spice coastal air of Vizag, or the haunting serenity of Varanasi's ghats at dawn, a Veranda property sits close enough to immerse you fully — yet far enough removed to give you perfect peace.

In The Spotlight

Three reasons to choose Veranda

Amid Lush Greenery

Amid Lush Greenery

Each Veranda property is cradled within — not placed upon — its natural surroundings. Ancient rain trees, rare orchid gardens, and biodiverse ecosystems form the living backdrop to your stay. Our horticulture teams maintain native species that have thrived here for centuries.

A Heritage Vibe

A Heritage Vibe

Founder Rajan Krishnamurthy's lifelong passion for preservation shaped every Veranda property. Vintage automobiles, antique grand pianos, and rare colonial-era artefacts are displayed — not archived — throughout each resort. History is not decoration here; it is the point.

Wellness & Rejuvenation

Wellness & Rejuvenation

Every Veranda property hosts a dedicated Serenity Spa drawing on Kerala Ayurveda traditions, trained therapists, and locally sourced botanical ingredients. Our wellness philosophy extends beyond the treatment room — it permeates the menu, the architecture, and the rhythm of each day.
